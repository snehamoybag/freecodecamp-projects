Life and technology change quickly. So does Ara.

The Ara frame contains all the functionality of a smartphone plus six flexible slots for easy swapping.

Ara modules fit neatly into the frame, allowing for upgrades, innovation and style.

Mix.Match.Swap

Slide any Ara module into any slot and it just works. Powering this simplicity is Greybus, a new bit of software deep in the Android stock. Greybus supports instantaneous connections, power efficiency and data-transfer rates of upto 11.9 Gbps.

Built to Last

The Ara frame is built with durable latches and connectors to keep modules secured. Ara modules are designed around standards, allowing them to new generations of frames and new form factors.
